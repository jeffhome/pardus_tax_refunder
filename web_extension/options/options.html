Tax Refunder Settings

» Orion
  • If a pilot doesn't have enough to pay the levy from the amount of taxes they have already paid, no levy will be taken from that pilot's tax refund, otherwise the levy will be deducted from the tax collected from them - and the remaining amount refunded.
» Artemis
  • If a pilot doesn't have enough to pay the levy from the amount of taxes they have already paid, no levy will be taken from that pilot's tax refund, otherwise the levy will be deducted from the tax collected from them - and the remaining amount refunded.
» Pegasus
  • If a pilot doesn't have enough to pay the levy from the amount of taxes they have already paid, no levy will be taken from that pilot's tax refund, otherwise the levy will be deducted from the tax collected from them - and the remaining amount refunded.
